Nethra Eye Care - Treatments for Eye Problems

Eye Exam

Comprehensive eye exams are essential for early detection and prevention of eye issues like cataracts, glaucoma, and refractive errors. At our Eye Care, advanced diagnostic tools are used to ensure a thorough assessment of your eye health, guiding personalized treatment options for better vision.

Cataract Surgery

Cataract surgery is one of the most effective ways to restore vision in those with cataracts. The procedure involves removing the clouded lens and replacing it with an intraocular lens (IOL). This advanced treatment helps regain clear sight and improves quality of life, reducing dependence on glasses.

Laser Eye Surgery

Laser eye surgeries like LASIK offer a permanent solution for refractive vision problems, including nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. Using precise laser technology, the cornea is reshaped to correct the focal point of light entering the eye, improving clarity without the need for corrective eyewear.
